This website has gone through a few different versions over the years, but the current version is built around Gatsby, MDX, and a design system that is simple enough to maintain without feeling too bare.

What I like about having my own site is that it gives me a place to experiment with layout, content structure, and small product decisions without needing a larger justification. It is a good home for blog posts, notes, and now project pages as well.

Like most long-running personal projects, it also acts as a record of how my preferences change over time. Some parts of it are about writing, some are about design, and some are simply about having a stable place on the web that I fully control.

This repo is less about a single feature and more about shaping an environment where different bits of work can live together coherently.
